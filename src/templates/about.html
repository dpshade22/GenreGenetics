About Spotify Genetics

Spotify Genetics is a web application that allows users to discover their music preferences and explore their favorite genres based on their Spotify listening history. By analyzing audio features such as energy, mood, tempo, and instrumentation, the application categorizes a user's top tracks and assigns a four-letter preference code to each track that represents the user's preference in terms of the four features.

Users can then explore their music preferences by genre and see their top tracks categorized by preference. The application also provides charts that visualize the user's preference distribution and recent tracks played.
